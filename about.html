Our story
Founded in early 2024, Dots is a young company bursting with fresh ideas. After working with Vadodara's finest architects, our founder saw the need for a firm that combines innovative design with high-quality aesthetics.
Our team excels in architecture, interior, and landscape design, ensuring a smooth process from concept to execution. Based in Vadodara, Gujarat, we focus on client-centric designs that transform spaces into stunning works of art. Our commitment to high-quality, fast, and reliable customer service ensures that every project exceeds expectations.

YASH SAJNANI

FOUNDER AND PRINCIPAL DESIGNER

Yash, the visionary behind Dots Design, is a passionate interior architect with 4 years of experience in creating timeless, functional spaces. Yash believes in transforming spaces to reflect the unique stories and lifestyles of clients, ensuring every project is a perfect harmony of innovation and elegance. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of design principles, he specialize in blending aesthetics with functionality to craft personalized environments.
